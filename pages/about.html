Veloura is a story of slow elegance, of uncovering perfection in the craft, and creating objects that naturally belong in your life.

The bag is the anchor to the silhouette.

The Veloura Philosophy

Our name is the promise of refinement. It stems from an obsession with the tactile universe. We design and curate bags that are equally practical as they are breathtaking.

We empower our community to make conscious choices through transparent supply chains and enduring designs. We are Veloura. Your essential canvas.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with tanneries that adhere strictly to sustainable practices. From plant-based dyes to water recycling systems, every piece of leather has a respectful genesis.

Architectural Integrity

We approach bag design like architecture. Form must support function perfectly. We pare away the unnecessary to reveal the core geometry that supports everyday life.
